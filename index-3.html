Middle School

What We're Built On

The Four Foundations of
Every Flourish School

01 — Character

Character

We grow the whole child. Curiosity, resilience, empathy, and self-awareness are not soft skills — they are the skills that determine whether a person truly flourishes in school and in life. We create space for students to deeply know themselves.

02 — Mastery

Mastery

Every student moves at their own pace, demonstrating genuine understanding before moving forward. Getting it right matters more than getting it done. Every student can reach mastery at the right time with the right support.

03 — Community

Community

School is not just a building you go to — it is a community you belong to. Every teacher knows every student. Every student knows each other. Families are actively included partners. The closeness is intentional and irreplaceable.

04 — Foundations

Foundations

Strong academic foundations in literacy, numeracy, and critical thinking open every future door. We take these seriously — not through drill and repetition, but through meaningful practice embedded in real projects and real questions.

Daily Life at Flourish

A Day in the Life

Every day is carefully designed for students to discover, question, and build with purpose.

8:00 AM
Morning Circle
Community building, biography studies of impactful people, personal & interpersonal skills. Students start every day connected and genuinely seen.
9:00 AM
Problem of the Day
A rigorous daily challenge pulled from the Flourish arc of learning. Students work up through a series of problems. Teachers pull small groups in real time when a gap is spotted.
10:00 AM
Foundations
AI-powered personalized lessons in reading, math, science and social studies. Mastery-based — every student on their own individual arc, never stuck or bored.
11:00 AM
Clubs & Lunch
Student-led clubs — golf, cooking, Roblox, sculpture, gardening and more. Students don't just join clubs, they plan and run them with teacher support.
12:00 PM
Novel Study
The class reads whole novels together. Diverse genres, protagonists and settings. Deep analysis, joyful discussion, and a genuine love of reading — every single day.
12:45 PM
Pursuits
Protected afternoon time for passion-based deep investigations. Students design their own studies into topics they genuinely care about. This is the heart of Flourish.
Project-Based Learning

Pursuits — Learning That
Actually Sticks

Extended, interdisciplinary projects where students tackle real questions that span math, writing, science, history and the arts — all at once. Learning that sticks because it means something real to the student.

🏞️

Design a Neighborhood Park

Skills Practiced

Math, geometry, budgeting, persuasive writing, and community advocacy — all woven into one real Pursuit.

Students present their designs to real Old Town Scottsdale community members and defend their choices with evidence.
📜

Investigate Local History

Skills Practiced

Primary source research, storytelling, data analysis, and oral presentation skills all come together naturally.

Old Town Scottsdale has incredible history. Students discover their own neighborhood in a completely new way.
🏪

Run a Student-Led Business

Skills Practiced

Economics, writing, math, teamwork, and real-world problem solving — learned entirely by doing, not reading.

Students pitch, plan, launch and reflect. Entrepreneurial thinking built from the inside out — in the heart of Scottsdale.

Every Pursuit is designed and owned by the student — with teacher guidance, real deliverables, and genuine accountability.
